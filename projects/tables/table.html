About Muse

Muse is a British rock band known for their energetic performances and genre-blending sound, incorporating elements of rock, electronic, and symphonic music. The trio, consisting of Matt Bellamy, Chris Wolstenholme, and Dominic Howard, is acclaimed for its concept-driven albums that explore themes of dystopia, politics, and existentialism. Since their debut in the late 1990s, Muse has built a dedicated fan base and earned multiple awards for their innovative style and powerful stage presence.
