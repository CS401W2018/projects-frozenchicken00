Interests - Muse

Interests Page | Hero Page

A British three-man rock band formed in 1994. The early genre was centered on alternatives similar to Radiohead's early albums. Currently, the band's own hard rock sensibilities are applied to Britpop. By 2016, it had sold more than 30 million albums worldwide.

Popular songs include Time Is Running Out, Plug In Baby, Supermassive Black Hole, Hysteria, Starlight, Uprise, Resistance, and Madness.

Their live performances are also spectacular, with elaborate stage designs and impressive light shows. Muse's music often explores themes of science fiction, politics, and personal struggles, which I find very engaging.
