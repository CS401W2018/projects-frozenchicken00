Shinnosuke Nohara

Shinnosuke, commonly known as Shin-chan, is a playful and mischievous five-year-old boy. He is known for his unique, sometimes inappropriate sense of humor and his carefree attitude. Shin-chan often gets into trouble because of his curious nature, but he has a big heart and cares deeply for his family and friends.

Misae Nohara

Misae, Shin-chan’s mother, is a stay-at-home mom who often struggles with managing her son’s wild antics. She is a loving and devoted mother but can be strict and hot-tempered, especially when Shin-chan causes trouble. Despite this, she deeply cares for her family and tries to teach Shin-chan valuable lessons.

Hiroshi Nohara

Hiroshi is Shin-chan’s father and works as a salaryman to support the family. He has a calm and easygoing personality, though he often finds himself overwhelmed by his son’s mischief and his wife’s strict nature. Hiroshi is hardworking and loves his family, even though he often becomes the target of Shin-chan’s playful jokes.

Himawari Nohara

Himawari is Shin-chan’s little sister and is still a toddler. Even at her young age, she has a strong fascination for shiny objects and handsome men, which often leads to humorous moments in the series. Like Shin-chan, she has a mischievous side, and her actions sometimes create funny situations for the whole family.
